---------------------------
swede_and_potato_boulangère
---------------------------

Serves- 4

Ingredients:
  ½ large swede, peeled, quartered & very thinly sliced
  4 medium/large potatoes, peeled & very finely sliced
  1 onion, finely sliced
  2 cloves garlic, crushed
  125ml hot vegetable stock
  125ml milk
  sea salt
  ground black pepper
  a little butter for greasing & dotting

Preheat the oven to 180°C.
In a large bowl, mix the swede, potatoes, onion and garlic together.
Season well with salt and pepper.
Grease a gratin or baking dish.
Spread the veg out evenly in the dish.
Pour over the stock and milk.
Dot with butter.
Bake for about 45-60 mins, depending on you oven, until the veg is cooked and has a brown crispy top.

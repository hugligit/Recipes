Serves- 8

Ingredients:
  2 lbs french green beans, ends trimmed
  Â¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
  3-4 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  1 tsp white miso
  1 cup basil leaves, finely chopped
  2-3 sprigs fresh parsley, finely chopped
  2-3 shallots, minced
  2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  1 fresh lemon's zest, grated
  4 small, ripe pears, halved and thinly sliced lengthwise (unpeeled)
  1 cup coarsely chopped walnut pieces, toasted

Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook beans for 5-6 minutes or until crisp-tender.
Drain well and set aside.

For the dressing whisk oil, vinegar, miso, basil, parsley, shallots, garlic and lemon zest until smooth.
Place beans, pears and walnut in a mixing bowl.
Toss gently with dressing and transfer to serving bowl.
Serve.

-----------------
Rice_Pudding_Oven
-----------------

Preheat oven to 160°C/Fan 140°C/Gas Mark 3.
Using 10g of salted butter lightly grease a large deep sided ovenproof dish, leave rest of the butter in the bottom of the dish.
Add 100g of rice, 25g of caster sugar and 750ml of semi-skimmed milk, stir well.
Bake in the centre of the preheated oven for 40 minutes.
Stir to separate the grains of rice.
Return to oven for a further 1 hour, unril a lightly browned skin has formed on the surface of the pudding.

cake

3/4 cup cup cocoa powder 3/4 cup flour 3/4 cup powdered sugar 1 cup lukewarm water 2-3 tsp canola oil 1-1/2 tsp baking soda 2-3 tsp white vinegar 2-3 tsp tsp vanilla essence

drizzle

4-5 tablespoon lukewarm water 1 and a 1/2 tablespoon coco powder 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Mix the ingredients softly and then pour it into a microwave safe glass bowl. Line the bowl with canola oil and cocoa powder.

Place the batter in regular microwave (heating mode) for 5-6 minutes only. Let it cool and serve it with the chocolate drizzle and some cherries or seasonal fruits.
